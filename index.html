TRUSU Computing Science Club

Welcome to the TRU Student Union Computing Science Club, the student club for undergratuates at Thompson Rivers University. We provide a social space, as well as academic support where everyone is welcome, not just computing science students. We also run events, such as our semesterly LAN party, and represent your interests to the Computing Science faculty.

COVID-19 Notice

Welcome to the Fall 2020 semester, where we are operating in an online-only capacity due to COVID-19! As you're aware, most if not all of your classes are being run online. So is the Computing Science Club!

We are working on opening up the Fall 2020 Help Lab on our Discord chatroom. If you're not in there, you really should be, as it will prove to be very valuable over the coming months.

You can find information on how to join our Discord chatroom here or by clicking "chatroom" above.

We look forward to spending the rest of the semester with you!
