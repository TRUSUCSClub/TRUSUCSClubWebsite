Support Lab

The Computer Science Club runs a semesterly support lab to help students needing some extra help and guidance in lower level classes with their assignments and topics. All the hours, and the current status, of the support lab can be found on this page.

Fall 2020

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, most classes are being delivered online. This is definitely a unique experience for many students.

In the Fall 2020 semester, we will be running a help lab through our Discord chatroom. Through this chatroom, you can either send text messages, or connect with a Support Lab volunteer through voice chat and screen share your problems.

As we work out details, we will be providing more information both on here and through Discord announcements.

Know your stuff? Volunteer with the Support Lab!

We are always looking for more support lab volunteers! If you can spare a small block of time per week and are willing to help other students, please volunteer with us!
