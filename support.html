Support Lab

The Computer Science Club runs a semesterly support lab to help students needing some extra help and guidance in lower level classes with their assignments and topics. All the hours, and the current status, of the support lab can be found on this page.

Winter 2020

The support lab is currently Closed on campus for the Winter 2020 semester due to the TRU campus shutdown. However, you can still get support online by joining our Discord chatroom! Find out how at https://trucsclub.ca/discord

Know your stuff? Volunteer with the Support Lab!

Currently, due to school closure the call for volunteers is now closed. However, if you're still interested in helping other students, please come to our Discord chatroom where we are continuing an online-only version of the help lab. Additionally, you can sign up for our mailing list to get notified when our in-person help lab re-opens.

Volunteer Survey

As a support lab volunteer, we'd appreciated if you could fill out the survey after each session. This helps the club track demand for course support so that we can better serve students.
